# avocado-snack
This is a delicious avocado snack.

## Ingredients

* A ripe avocado
* Sea Salt
* Fresh ground black pepper
* Olive oil
* Basalmic Vinegar

## Directions

* Get the avocado out of the shell and into a bowl
* Sprinkle salt and pepper to your liking
* Pour on olive oil
* Pour on basalmic
* Eat.
